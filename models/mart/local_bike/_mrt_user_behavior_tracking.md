This model provides insights into the revenue generated by each customer, including details related to the product and user attributes
- **customer_id**: customer_id associated with the revenue.
- **customer_city**: city associated with the customer_id and the revenue.
- **customer_state**: state associated with the customer_id and the revenue.
- **product_name**: product_name associated with the customer_id and the revenue.
- **brand_name**: brand_name associated with the customer_id and the revenue.
- **category_name**: product category_name associated with the customer_id and the revenue.
- **model_year**: Product model release year
- **total_revenue**: Revenue computed as the sum of revenues from the stg_local_bike__order_items model
